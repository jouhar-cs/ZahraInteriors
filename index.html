about us

Our Story

Zahra is the vision of its founder Nizam Shahid, who lived and studied in Karnataka, India, before embarking on a voyage of discovery around the world.

Our interior design company draws on this rich source of design inspiration, bringing the best of international design philosophy to its practice, realised in instantly recognizable elegant and livable interiors. Lumi partners with a stable of exceptional suppliers and artisans

our services

Zahra works on a multitude of different projects, from private residential to large scale hospitality, from contemporary to classic, whilst honoring its commitment to excellence and sound design principles.

All projects are created using the finest materials and finishes, in collaboration with the most talented artisans and the finest manufacturers in their field. The team manages projects end to end, ensuring the process is seamless and enjoyable.

Interior Architecture
Our experienced interior architects seamlessly handle every aspect of interior architectural design from the strategic arrangement of space planning, fitted joinery.

FF&E Design
Zahra team of talented interior designers provides a comprehensive range from soft furnishings to bespoke furniture and lighting design, accompanied by artwork selection.

Design Project Management
We carefully monitor the progress of all design elements on-site to ensure precise implementation, conducting regular site visits and coordinating with subcontractors and third parties.

Styling
Zahra offers an end-to-end styling service, to include the complete accessorizing and dressing of homes, for clients. Zahra team is adept at sourcing art, antiques, sculpture and other ornaments and rare objects for clients.
